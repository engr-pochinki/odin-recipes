Chicken Adobo Recipe

Chicken Adobo is a Filipino staple dish.

Ingredients

  • chicken
  • soy sauce
  • vinegar
  • garlic
  • sugar
  • laurel
  • pepper
  • water

Steps:

  1. Put the chopped up chicken on a wok.
  2. Pour appropriate amounts of soy sauce and vinegar. Let boil.
  3. When the chicken are tender, add soy sauce or vinegar to taste.
  4. Balance taste with sugar and water. Once satisfied, let boil again.
  5. After a few minutes, add pepper and laurel. Boil again.
  6. After another few minutes, simmer and serve.
