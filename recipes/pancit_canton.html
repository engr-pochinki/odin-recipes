Pancit Canton Recipe

Pancit canton is a Pinoy dormer staple.

Ingredients

  • Lucky Me! Pancit Canton flavor of choice
  • water
  • Optional: Egg

Steps:

  1. Bring water to a boil.
  2. Pour noodles to boiling water.
  3. Strain water from the noodles after 3 minutes.
  4. Pour packet contents to noodles.
  5. Serve.
